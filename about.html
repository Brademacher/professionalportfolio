About Me

Hello! My name is Ben. I recently graduated from a 24 week full-stack coding bootcamp run by the University of Minnesota College of Continuing Education and Professional Studies, and am currently seeking a position where I can become a better developer through my own practice, code reviews, and mentorship from senior staff.

The program focused on MERN stack development, but incorporated several other technologies and libraries. I work hard to understand new materials, while continuing to practice and hone my technical skills in hopes of becoming an asset to a professional development team.

I started this journey into development and computer science what feels like ages ago, when I originally entered a computer science program at the University of Minnesota Duluth way back in 2004. While I did not finish my degree during my first go around of school, I learned the importance of hard work and dedication; which I bring to every job. I still intend on finishing my degree, but wanted to get into development as quickly as possible - hence enrolling in the bootcamp.

Since graduating, I continue my studies by watching videos (The Coding Train and Traversy Media are two of my favorite channels), do practice problems on codewars.com, and reading books recommended by friends in the industry. I'm pretty I'm losing friends, because I'll randomly bring up coding to non-developers all the time now. I need a true outlet for my IT nerdiness!
